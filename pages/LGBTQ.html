Did You Know?

The mental health of LGBTQ youth is a topic of great concern and importance. These individuals often face unique challenges related to their sexual orientation or gender identity, including stigma, discrimination, and a lack of acceptance from peers and family members. These experiences can lead to higher rates of depression, anxiety, and suicide attempts among LGBTQ youth compared to their heterosexual and cisgender counterparts.

  • Providing mental health care can significantly reduce the chance of suicide
