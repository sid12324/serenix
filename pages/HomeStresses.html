Did You Know?

Domestic abuse has profound and long-lasting effects on youth who are exposed to such traumatic situations within their families. Witnessing or experiencing domestic violence can have a devastating impact on their mental and emotional well-being. Children and adolescents living in homes marked by abuse may develop anxiety, depression, and post-traumatic stress disorder. They often struggle with feelings of guilt, fear, and low self-esteem.
