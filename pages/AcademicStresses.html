Did You Know?

The mental health of students is a critical concern in today's fast-paced and high-pressure educational environments. Students often grapple with academic stress, social pressures, and the challenges of transitioning into adulthood. The demands of schoolwork, extracurricular activities, and the constant use of digital devices can lead to anxiety, depression, and burnout. It's important for educational institutions to prioritize the mental well-being of their students by offering resources such as counseling services, stress management programs, and mental health education.
